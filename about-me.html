Hello and welcome to my website. My name is Ross McIntyre and I am a web developer / software engineer.

I am currently employed as a Geotechnical Engineer, a career I have been involved with for the last 7 years since graduating with a degree in Geology. As part of my role I have to assess large datasets and make calculations based upon that data. The company I worked for utilised outdated methods which involved manually completing these calculations. I developed an interest in automating that work, which was simply done with Excel functions. This project lead me to an interest in software development and I have been teaching myself web development and software engineering since mid 2020, through various resources such as Free Code Camp and Udemy.

To advance my knowledge and skills, I am currently enrolled on the University of Manchester Full Stack Web Development Coding Bootcamp and I am due to graduate this course in March 2021. The course is part time during evenings and weekends and I will be full time employed whilst undertaking the course. The course content includes HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, jQuery, Bootstrap, Express.js, React.js, Node.js, MongoDB and MySQL.

I have experience in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, jQuery, Node.js, Express.js, mongoDB and Python. Please view my Portfolio to see the various projects I have been working on.
